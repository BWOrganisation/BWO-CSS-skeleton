BWO VISION STATEMENT
Our vision is to value dignity and uniqueness of hopeless children within the community we serve and envision to build the community and family in any aspect of life for self-reliance.

Best Wing Organization

OUR GOAL - OUR APPROACH

OUR GOAL

Contribute to the wellbeing of the most vulnerable children (boys and girls) and their families and communities, allowing them to experience life in all its fullness in future.

OUR APPROACH

During the implementation of its activities, Best wing organization uses a community based approach and works closely with children, families, communities, local authorities and partners. Best wing organization listens to the families and their concerns so that the support they get will produce long/ lasting changes for the children. We work in one province through four integrated development programs (IDP). In addition, we have advocacy and participation.

BWO Core Values

Dignity,Good stewardship,Empowerment

Dignity

it’s our culture that every person has right to be valued and respected for their own sake, and to be treated ethically.

Good stewardship

we take well informed decisions, monitor and supervise.

Empowerment

we do advocacy, empower people to be confident, realize their God given gifts/potentials, and create their own income generating activities.

Background

Best Wing Organization (BWO) is a Christian relief, development, and advocacy organization with operations in Rwanda, Best Wing Organization was founded in 2020.Its Mission is to Promote the development and survival of the most vulnerable children by observing their basic human rights and help them and their families to be self-reliant. General objective of BWO is to promote sustainable development in the community through promotion of health, education, child protection, gender and social inclusion, and socio economic development through activities as trainings, advocacy, competitions, community dialogues and campaigns.
